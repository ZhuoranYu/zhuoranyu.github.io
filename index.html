About Me

My name is Zhuoran Yu and I am a Senior Research Scientist at Autodesk, where I work on universal foundation models for 3D content generation and design intelligence. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UW-Madison, advised by Prof. Yong Jae Lee. Previously, I obtained my Masters in Computer Science from Georgia Institute of Technology where I worked with Prof. Zsolt Kira. My research broadly focuses on multimodal foundation models, data-centric learning, and benchmarks/evaluation for visual and multimodal reasoning.
